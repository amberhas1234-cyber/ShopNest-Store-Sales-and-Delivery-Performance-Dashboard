# ShopNest-Store-Sales-and-Delivery-Performance-Dashboard

### 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard built using Power BI to analyze the ShopNest retail dataset. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales performance, delivery efficiency, customer behavior, product ratings, and revenue trends.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights that support data-driven decision-making.

### 🔍 Key Business Questions & Insights
##### 🛍️ Top Product Categories
- Identified the top 10 categories by total sales
- Lifestyle-related categories (health, sports, etc.) drive the highest revenue
- Helps in inventory planning and promotional strategies
#### 🚚 Delivery Performance Analysis
- Compared actual vs estimated delivery dates to detect delays
- Identified categories with higher delay rates
- Key factors influencing delays: 
Logistics complexity, 
Inventory availability, 
Shipping distance
#### 📅 Monthly Delivery Trends
- Compared on-time vs delayed orders over time
Found:
On-time deliveries dominate overall,
Seasonal spikes in delays,
Increasing order volume → business growth,
Drillthrough enabled detailed order-level insights
#### 💳 Payment Method Analysis
- Credit cards are the most preferred payment method
Followed by boleto, vouchers, and debit cards
Useful for:
Payment optimization,
Customer-centric offers
#### ⭐ Product Rating Analysis
Identified:
- Top 10 highest-rated products
- Bottom 10 lowest-rated products
Insights support:
- Product quality improvements
- Supplier evaluation
- Better customer experience
#### 🌍 State-wise Sales Performance
- Revenue distribution across regions
- High-performing vs low-performing states identified
Enables:
Targeted marketing,
Regional expansion strategies
#### 📈 Seasonal & Quarterly Trends
- Strong upward sales trend (2016–2018)
- Identified seasonal demand fluctuations
Helps in:
Forecasting, 
Campaign planning, 
Inventory management
#### 💰 Yearly Revenue Growth
- Consistent increase in total revenue over time
Indicates:
Growing customer base, 
Increased order volume, 
Strong business scalability
